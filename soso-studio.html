About Soso Studio

Soso Studio is a creative rug focused website that centers around workshop hosting and personalized rug commissions. It serves as both a hub for rug enthusiasts and a trustworthy brand platform, offering valuable information on rug categories, care techniques, and FAQs. Through its visually cohesive design and clear navigation, Soso Studio creates a space that blends artistry with reliability encouraging users to explore, learn, and create within the rug-making community.

My Contributions

My main contributions to Soso Studio include designing the entire mobile layout and selecting the cohesive color palette used across the site. I created most of the Index Page and developed the initial design framework that guided the project's overall look and feel. Additionally, I contributed numerous design ideas throughout the development process, collaborated closely with teammates to refine features and designed the complete Rug Care page ensuring both visual consistency and user-friendly functionality across the platform.

Soso Studio Website Overview

The Index Page serves as the welcoming heart of Soso Studio, showcasing standout rugs that reflect the brand's craftsmanship while guiding users toward upcoming workshops and linking to detailed sessions. A live Instagram feed adds authenticity by displaying the studio's latest projects and community updates. The Custom Page offers an interactive rug design tool where users can experiment with canvas shapes, upload images, add text, draw, and customize colors with undo, redo, and reset options for flexibility. Meanwhile, the Workshops Page lists all available sessions with key details and a "Book Now" link to ClassBento, making it easy for visitors to explore, design, and engage with Soso Studio's creative experiences.
