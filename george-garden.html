About George Garden

George Garden Centre is a vibrant and nature-inspired website designed to reflect the beauty and freshness of a modern garden shop. It features a calming green color palette, organic textures and clean layouts that evoke a sense of growth and tranquility. The website serves as an online hub for garden enthusiasts to explore a wide range of plants, pots, garden tools and fertilizers while learning more about the brand's passion for sustainable gardening and customer care.

My Contributions

I designed and developed the George Garden Centre website as part of my class assessment project. My main contributions include building a responsive, mobile-first layout and implementing a carousel on the landing page to showcase featured products. I also created a contact form with a dropdown menu to help users specify their gardening interests, added embedded YouTube videos for tutorials and developed a product gallery featuring 20 different items across multiple categories. Throughout the project, I focused on creating an intuitive user experience and maintaining a cohesive "green organic" aesthetic.

George Garden Website Overview

The website consists of multiple pages, including a home page with an image carousel and product highlights, a detailed product gallery categorized by plant types, a contact page with a functional form and an information page featuring gardening videos. Designed with a mobile-first approach, each page adapts smoothly to various screen sizes, ensuring usability on both desktop and mobile devices. The layout emphasizes simplicity, accessibility and visual appeal, successfully bringing together all the required elements of the assessment while showcasing a professional, nature-themed website design.
