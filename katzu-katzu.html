About Katzu Katzu

Katzu Katzu is a modern Japanese restaurant specialising in comforting and flavourful dishes, inspired by authentic Japanese cuisine. The website reflects the restaurant's bold and energetic brand through strong colours, clean layouts, and engaging visuals. It is designed to give visitors a clear overview of the restaurant, its menu, and its atmosphere while remaining easy to navigate and visually appealing.

My Contributions

I designed and developed the Katzu Katzu website from concept to completion. My work included building the responsive layout, creating dynamic menu sections, implementing sliders for featured content, and styling the site to match the brand's strong visual identity. I also ensured mobile responsiveness, consistent typography, and smooth interactions across all pages to deliver a professional and user-friendly experience.

Katzu Katzu Website Overview

The homepage introduces Katzu Katzu with bold visuals and structured content sections that highlight the restaurant's identity and offerings. The Menu page presents food categories in an organised and easy-to-browse layout, allowing users to quickly explore available dishes. The website uses strong colour contrasts, modern typography, and subtle animations to enhance engagement. The Contact page provides clear restaurant information and an accessible contact form, making it easy for customers to get in touch or plan their visit. Overall, the website delivers a strong and cohesive digital presence that represents Katzu Katzu's modern Japanese dining experience.
