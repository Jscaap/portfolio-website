About Hatch Cafe

Hatch Cafe is a cozy and modern dessert café specializing in signature egg waffles and handcrafted drinks. The website captures the brand’s warm, minimalist aesthetic with soft colors, elegant typography and smooth animations that reflect the café’s relaxing atmosphere. Visitors can explore the café’s story, menu highlights and seasonal specials while enjoying a visually appealing and easy-to-navigate design that mirrors the real cafe experience.

My Contributions

I designed and developed the Hatch Cafe website, focusing on creating a seamless and interactive user experience. My main contributions include building the responsive layout, implementing Swiper sliders for the Trending Flavours section and adding scroll-triggered animations for each content block. I also ensured mobile optimization, consistent branding across all pages and smooth visual transitions that enhance user engagement.

Hatch Cafe Website Overview

The homepage introduces users to Hatch Cafe’s unique concept, blending elegant visuals with dynamic Swiper sliders that showcase popular menu items. The Menu Page highlights categories like Eggchi waffles, drinks and desserts, presented through interactive carousels. Each section is designed with a focus on modern café aesthetics—featuring warm tones, stylish fonts and subtle animations. The Contact Page includes a clean, accessible form and map integration, making it simple for customers to reach out or visit in person. Altogether, the Hatch Cafe website delivers an inviting and modern digital experience that matches the cafe’s creative and comforting brand identity.
